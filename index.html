About Sisters in Tech

Sisters in Tech is a community-led initiative dedicated to increasing women's participation in technology. We run mentorship, training, and career programs for women of all levels.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to empower women in technology through education, mentorship, and community support. We envision a world where women thrive in tech careers and leadership roles. where women are equally represented in all tech fields.

We are committed to creating a supportive environment that fosters growth, collaboration, and innovation among women in tech.

Our vision is to create a world where women are empowered to pursue their passions in technology and achieve their career goals.
